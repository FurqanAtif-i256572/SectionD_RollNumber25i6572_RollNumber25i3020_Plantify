OUR JOURNEY

Our Story:

More Than Just Plants

PLANTIFY Commerce was born from a simple, personal realization: filling a home with plants is easy, but keeping them thriving is a challenge. Our founder, Mutahir Ali , a former urban gardener, watched too many well-meaning plant lovers struggle with wilting leaves and unanswered questions. In 2020, he started PLANTIFY from his own sunlit apartment, pairing each plant with simple, clear care guides. What began as a local delivery service has blossomed into a national community of over 50,000 plant parents, united by a shared joy for nurturing green life. We're not just selling plants; we're cultivating confidence.

Our mission:

Is to make plant care accessible and rewarding for everyone. We deliver curated, resilient plants directly to you, paired with simple tools and knowledge, to cultivate greener homes and clearer minds.
